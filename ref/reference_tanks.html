KV1-S

PROS

  • Above average speed and maneuverability.
  • 122mm is one of the most powerful guns at tier 6

CONS

  • Low radio signal and view range - don't get too far away from team members.
  • Very slow reload and poor aim time on 122mm, inaccurate at long range.
  • Lowest hitpoint pool of all tier 6 heavies. Avoid brawls.

EQUIPMENT

  • Rammer
  • Vents
  • Coated Optics

STRATEGY

It can outrun most of its same tier kin and thus enables it to reach positions other tanks would have trouble reaching before the enemy does.

SU-122-44

PROS

  • Good mobility and traverse - above average speed
  • Good damage (122mm gun)
  • Good angle and front armor - can engage head-on
  • Rate of fire
  • Low profile
  • Signal range - 700m

CONS

  • Accuracy - not that great for long distance sniping. Keep under 400 m
  • Low Penetration
  • View Range 330m - again, not a long-range sniper.
  • Aim time
  • Expensive ammo. Don't waste shots.

EQUIPMENT

  • Gun Rammer
  • GLD
  • Optics or Binocs
  • Vents

SKILLS and PERKS

  • Camo
  • Sixth Sense

STRATEGY

Hang behind front line at beginning then use mobility to flank.

Find cover. Take a shot, back off to reload.
