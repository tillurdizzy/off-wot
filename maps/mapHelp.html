Customize

Add your own personal markers to the map

  1. You must be logged in.
  2. Click the Add Marker button
  3. Select the spawn location (red or green)
  4. Select the class of tank (Light, Medium, Heavy, TD or SPG)
  5. Click once on the map to place your marker
  6. Click Reject to try again, or Save to keep it.
  7. To delete a marker, click on it, then select Delete.
