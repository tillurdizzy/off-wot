Overview

There are several areas in Airfield that demand a team's attention in order to secure victory. The runway is typically home to a long-distance shootout, as cover is sparse and line-of-sight is good. Take cover behind outcroppings and boulders and exchange volleys with the opposition. Wear them down and control the north.

The center high ground is perhaps the most important area on the map, as any players positioned there often can get clear shots. Be prepared to brawl since the opposing team can is likely to meet you there. If your tank is fortunate enough to have decent gun depression, take position at a vantage point, take the enemy out and win the day.

The south of the map provides good scouting opportunities, but the enemy will also be looking for you there. Be stealthy and let your teammates know where the enemy is moving; you can turn the tide of the battle.
