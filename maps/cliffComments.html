Quick Strategy

Heat maps show that the South spawn is generally more successful in advancing on both East and West sides. To counter the South's advantage, North must get into offensive positions very quickly through the valley and around the lighthouse.

Lights

Lights generally favor the center of the map and the lighthouse hill.

Mediums

Mediums generally favor the center of the map and the lighthouse hill.

Destroyers

TD's can follow the heavies, or take up sniping positions in the town from south spawn, or in the hills for north spawn.

Heavies

Heavies can go east, center or west, but should stick together in packs of 2-3.

Summary

Starting in the north and south, both teams are connected through a valley on the extreme west of the map. Large boulders offer cover south of the valley, and a small town provides cover to the north. The eastern portion of the map is elevated, with two large jutting hills providing cover from enemy fire. An overlooking position near the center of the map allows tanks to fire into the valley from above. Plentiful cover throughout the area allow for multiple attack routes, with careful coordination and flanking playing a significant role.
