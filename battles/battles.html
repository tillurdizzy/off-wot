Fart Score

Scoring is based on the Battle Tier/Leader Board Position combination, with Bottom Tier/First on Leader Board being the highest possible score, and Top Tier/Bottom 5 being the lowest score.
Your score may be quite eratic until you have recorded 20-30 battles, then it will even out.
40 or Above: Excellent
30-40: Good
20-30: Average
20 or Below: Needs Improvement

Return to Garage
